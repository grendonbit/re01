About Us


Energy, Materials and Nanotechnology(EMN) Summit Shimmer Center, established from the Interdisciplinary Materials and Devices (IMD) team of IFFS, UESTC, comprises a highly accomplished research team. Its research encompasses several key areas, including perovskite optoelectronic materials and devices, the design and fabrication of semiconductor quantum dots for energy and optoelectronic applications, mid-short wave infrared imaging and focal plane detector modules, single-molecule fluorescence systems for tumor visualization, flexible optoelectronic sensors, plasmon-enhanced signal detection, and photonic nuclear medicine information intelligence, among others.

Tianfu Jiangxi Laboratory is dedicated to advancing information technology, focu-sing on achieving seamless connectivity across various research areas. These include optical information technology, photonics, functional materials and com-ponents, optical sensing technology, and integrating quantum and neuromorphic computing with artificial intelligence.
The lab aims to foster groundbreaking scientific and technological advancements that could redefine the future. It targets overcoming challenges in the technical progression from materials to devices, systems, and applications. By expediting the translation of local scientific discoveries and technological innovations, the laboratory aims to propel strategic industrial advancements in the field of information technology for futuristic development.

Shimmer Center, established from the Interdisciplinary Materials and Devices (IMD) team of IFFS, UESTC, comprises a highly accomplished research team.
