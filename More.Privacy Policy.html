         Privacy Policy


    This is the privacy policy for the websites of #, which are all maintenanced and sponsored by Shimmer center and Tianfu Jiangxi Laboratory.


    We are committed to protecting the privacy of authors, participants, reviewers, volunteers, members and all the other contacts. Below you will find information we may collect and how we use your personal data.


    Information We May Collect


    During the process of the summit, paper submission, conference registration, peer review and other related activities, we may have collected the following information:


Personal name and contact details
Education information
Area of expertise
peer review information
Purchasing and payment information
Information about service usage
Cookies
Location information
Other information you upload or provide to us


    Information We May Use to Do


    We use your personal information for the following purposes:


    To advertise for future EMN Summit events. You may receive multiple call-for-papers of the future EMN Summit events if you have registered. If you are not willing to be disturbed, you can select to UNSUBSCRIBE from our advertisement.


    To communicate with your paper submission or EMN Summit participation.If you have submitted manuscripts to EMN Summit events, we will use your personal data to contact you.


    To request for peer review process. Sometimes, as a suggested reviewer, your personal information may be provided by authors, colleagues, editors and etc.. We may use your personal and professional information in our database to judge whether you will be requested as a reviewer.


    To update the EMN Summit membership or award evaluation. Information will be also submitted to EMN Summit Award Committee. Author who has participated EMN Summit events will be recorded as a EMN Summit member. EMN Summit will evaluate the personal and professional information of all members after every events. Information of qualified members will be submitted to Fellow Committees for further evaluation.


    We reserve the right to update this policy from time to time by updating our website, respectively. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.


    This policy was last updated on May 31, 2024.
